About

Rooted in Research,
Driven by Impact

I am a Computer Science researcher and PhD student interested in designing and studying technologies that create meaningful social impact. My current work focuses on privacy and security in EdTech platforms, AI and LLM governance, and the policy, ethical, and sociotechnical challenges that shape how large-scale digital systems are designed and deployed. I am especially interested in how privacy policies, governance frameworks, and ethical principles translate into real-world technical practice.

Beyond research, I'm a public speaker, educator, and advocate for diversity and inclusion in STEM. I always welcome challenges with curiosity and a commitment to doing work that genuinely matters.
