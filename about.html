ABOUT ME

Hello there! I am Meghna Manoj Nair and Welcome to My Blog. I'm a 20 year old, ordinary mundane and I love adventures and surprises and this makes me a vibrant and exuberant individual. A third-year Computer Science Student pursuing my B.Tech Course in VIT Chennai, I have worked as an Avionics Engineer at Team Aviators International, Research and Innovation Lead at AUVSI Chennai and as the Electrical and Programming Lead of Humanoid Club and I love exlporing topics which form the essence of a magical combination which includes AI/ML, Robotics, Blockchain, Cyber Physical Systems, etc. and have also published Research Papers on the same. Public speaking is my passion and it has been very close to my heart for a very long time. A microphone and stage would get me plunged into my world of ideas and imaginations and I would keep the audience held. Speeaking at one of the TEDx events has always topped my bucket list, and it still does. Apart from academics and public speaking, I engross myself in dancing, horse-riding, flying RC planes and drones, reading, creative writing, etc. My vision in life is to learn and let learn by reaching out to those communities and societies out there who hardly have any access to the amazing opportunities that the tech world offers!
